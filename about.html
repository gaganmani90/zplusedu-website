About ZPlusEdu

Meet the mentors dedicated to your growth

Our Mission

Personalized Guidance for Real Growth

ZPlusEdu was founded with a simple belief: the right mentorship at the right time can change the trajectory of a career. We provide customized guidance for students and working professionals through remote sessions, in-person meetings, and ad-hoc consultations.

Hundreds of students and professionals have gone through our program — and we're just getting started.
